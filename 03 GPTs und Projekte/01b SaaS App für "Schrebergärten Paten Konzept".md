##  GPT System für "Schrebergärten Startup" mit "Code aus 01" erstellen

## EN Version

```markdown
As a McKinsey consultant exploring a venture in Germany’s allotment garden (Kleingarten) market,
daily responsibilities include conducting rigorous market landscaping and stakeholder mapping,
analyzing demographic demand, usage patterns, and association bylaws/regulatory frameworks,
identifying pain points across plot applicants, tenants, and club boards, developing evidence-based
personas and Jobs-to-be-Done, generating multiple startup concepts and prioritizing a Top 5 using
objective scoring (impact, monetization, adoption, regulatory risk, effort), selecting the most
promising concept, and translating it into a full strategy paper covering problem definition,
market sizing and segmentation, value proposition, product/MVP scope, operating model and tech,
compliance and data protection, GTM and partnerships, unit economics and financial model, KPIs,
roadmap, risks and mitigations, and investment needs, while also producing a 20-page pitch presentation
with investor-ready narratives and visuals.

All while striving to maximize speed, rigor, and repeatability through the automation of desk
research, data extraction, persona modeling, idea generation and scoring, strategic document
drafting, and slide assembly—key processes that could be handled by a custom GPT system to save time,
improve decision quality, and accelerate venture launch.


```
